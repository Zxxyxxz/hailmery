## The selling mindset

You are selling a **tireless, brilliant, cost-effective new member of the security team: an AISecOps Autonomous Workforce** powered by 5 Specialized AI Agents. Move every conversation away from tactical features and into strategic solutions. Frame everything around this value.

## Module 1 — The "Why" (your opening move)

Open by listening for the **three universal pains**:

- **People Headache:** "We can't hire enough security experts." / "Our team is burned out."
- **Money Headache:** "These scanning tools charge us per asset and it's killing us."
- **Complexity Headache:** "I have ten different dashboards and thousands of alerts."

## Module 2 — The elevator pitch (the "What")

> "In simple terms, OSM provides an **AISecOps Autonomous Workforce**. It's an agentic AI security team powered by **5 Specialized AI Agents** that work 24/7 to continuously find and fix your security risks before hackers can. Think about the cost of hiring a top-tier security team — OSM gives you that brainpower, supported by deep **OSM AI Memory**, for a fraction of the cost."

## Module 3 — The use-case plays (the "How to Sell It")

**Play #1 — The Overwhelmed & Understaffed Team**
> "OSM is your AISecOps Autonomous Workforce. For small businesses, it's an entire security team in a box. For large enterprises, the 5 Specialized Agents are the ultimate force multiplier — handling repetitive data analysis so humans focus on strategic threats."

**Play #2 — The Budget-Constrained CFO / IT Director**
> "OSM bundles powerful Continuous Offensive Security with zero extra cost for unlimited scanning. You eliminate a huge line item and add an incredibly smart autonomous brain to analyze results."

**Play #3 — The CISO with Too Many Dashboards**
> "OSM is your single Executive Command Center for risk. It breaks down silos, pulling Network, Web, Containers, and Code into one unified screen. The AI Risk Manager connects the dots for true visibility."

**Play #4 — The Security-Conscious Government Client**
> "OSM deploys as an On-Premise Air-Gapped AI appliance with OSM AI Memory — your data never leaves the network."

## Licensing & sales process

- Scales from **Platinum subscription** (Network + Web) up to **full Enterprise licensing** (adds manual penetration testing features).
- Deliver **Proof of Concepts quickly** using the integrated/bundled scanners (Scanner VA, operational in 15 minutes).
- **PoC requirements** are provided as a separate document (per the Turkish brief).

## Strategic recommendations (marketing)

Position OSM as the comprehensive **CTEM platform executed via the Executive Command Center**. Lead with the AI differentiators (**5 Specialized AI Agents + OSM AI Memory**), demonstrate **Continuous Offensive Security over periodic scanning**, and highlight the **massive ROI** of an autonomous workforce.
