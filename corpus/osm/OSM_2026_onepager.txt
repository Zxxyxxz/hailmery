WHY NOW? THE 2026 STATE OF EMERGENCY

The numbers tell the truth:
• 40,000+ new vulnerabilities discovered every year
• Across 4 layers of attack surface (Network, Web, Container, Source Code)
• Across hundreds or thousands of organizational assets

Cybersecurity is now a Big Data problem—a data volume no human team can process. And the adversary has changed: your enemies are no longer human hackers. They are AI engines processing Big Data to find and exploit weaknesses at machine speed.

The proof is alarming. In the React2Shell incident, 77,000 IPs were breached in just 2 days. According to Mandiant M-Trends 2025, it now takes only 48 hours to breach after exploit release. Organizations relying on weekly or monthly scans are defenseless—if an exploit drops on Wednesday, they will be breached by Friday, days before their next scheduled scan.

The 2026 axiom: You can only defend against AI with AI.

WHAT IS OSM?

OSM is the vendor-independent Continuous Threat Exposure Management (CTEM) platform that unifies offensive security data across Network, Web Application, Container, and Source Code layers into a single AI-powered command center. OSM is the "Collision Sensor" against cyber threats in IT—detecting, analyzing, and responding to risks before they become breaches.

HOW OSM WORKS

OSM operates across 6 integrated pillars:

1. Multi-Layer Risk Visibility — Manage Network, Web, Container, and Source Code risks in a single vendor-independent platform.
2. AI-Powered Risk Management — Enrich, manage, prioritize, and communicate risks with the power of OSM AI.
3. Proactive Data Analytics — OSM analyzes every risk information available, fusing Security Data + Asset Composition + Security Context in real-time to prevent attacks. Better than human. Faster than human.
4. Continuous Threat Exposure Management — Real-time, always-on monitoring replaces periodic scanning to beat the 48-hour exploit window.
5. Offensive Testing & Reporting — Automated and manual penetration testing with zero-touch compliance report generation.
6. Risk Management & Governance — Unified process management for SLAs, escalations, notifications, and stakeholder accountability.
