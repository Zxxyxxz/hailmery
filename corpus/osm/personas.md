# OSM — Buyer Personas & Pains

**What this file contains:** The buyer personas, their pains, and the value proposition + messaging hook for each. Grounded in the OSM source documents (especially the sales playbooks and the comprehensive sales/marketing doc). Use this to target generated content to the right audience.

**Sources:** `The OSM Sales Playbook_ Selling the Agentic AI Security Team.txt`, `The OSM Sales Playbook_ Selling the Virtual AI Security Team (1).txt`, `Comprehensive Sales & Marketing Documentation_ OSM and APIRE.txt`, `Understanding Offensive Security Manager (OSM)_*.txt`.

The profiles below are reconstructed faithfully from that material; do not invent named individuals.

## The three universal "headaches" (the pain framework)

Every OSM conversation opens by listening for one of three pains:

1. **The People Headache** — "We can't hire enough security experts." / "Our team is burned out." Skills/resources gap + alert fatigue.
2. **The Money Headache** — "These scanning tools charge us per asset and it's killing us." High cost of tools + experts; asset-based licensing.
3. **The Complexity Headache** — "I have ten different dashboards and thousands of alerts." Tool/data silos, fragmented visibility, blind spots.

---

## Persona 1 — The CISO (Chief Information Security Officer)

- **Pains:** Too many dashboards; can't see true risk posture; must report to the board; accountable for breaches.
- **Value proposition:** Strategic risk reduction and board-level reporting via the **Executive Command Center**. The **AI Risk Manager** connects the dots across Network, Web, Container, and Code for true, unified visibility and a **Real Risk Score**.
- **Hook:** "OSM is your single Executive Command Center for risk. It breaks down silos and gives the board a true, business-aligned risk picture."

## Persona 2 — The Security Operations Manager / Overwhelmed & Understaffed Team

- **Pains:** Understaffed SOC; analyst burnout; thousands of alerts; can't keep up with the 48-hour window.
- **Value proposition:** Operational excellence with the **AISecOps Autonomous Workforce** as a **force multiplier** — 5 Specialized AI Agents handle repetitive data analysis and triage up to **94%** of noise, freeing humans for strategy. For small teams, OSM is "an entire security team in a box."
- **Hook:** "OSM is your AISecOps Autonomous Workforce, working 24/7 — a force multiplier for large teams, an entire SOC for small ones."

## Persona 3 — DevOps / Security Engineers (DevSecOps)

- **Pains:** Security slows down delivery; scanning friction; findings don't reach the right people.
- **Value proposition:** **Continuous Offensive Security** embedded directly in **CI/CD** (Jenkins, GitLab) without velocity friction; two-way **Jira** sync; OWASP-based Web App AI builds dev-ready action plans.
- **Hook:** "Security that keeps pace with your pipeline — Agentic AI in your DevSecOps stack, no velocity tax."

## Persona 4 — Compliance Officers

- **Pains:** Audit readiness; manual control validation; proving continuous posture.
- **Value proposition:** **Zero-touch compliance reporting** and continuous control validation/tracking against **PCI DSS (223 controls), SOC 2, OWASP, PSD2, MSTG, ISO 27001, NIS2, GDPR**.
- **Hook:** "Audit-ready, continuously — automated control validation mapped to your frameworks."

## Persona 5 — The Budget-Constrained CFO / IT Director

- **Pains:** Stretched budget; expensive asset-based licensing; difficult trade-offs between coverage and cost.
- **Value proposition:** OSM **bundles unlimited scanning at zero extra cost** and adds an autonomous AI brain — eliminating a huge line item while increasing coverage. Massive ROI via tool consolidation (4X) and breach-cost avoidance.
- **Hook:** "Eliminate the per-asset scanning bill and add a tireless AI analyst team — for a fraction of the cost of one senior hire."

## Persona 6 — The Security-Conscious Government / Military Client

- **Pains:** Data sovereignty; cannot send data to the cloud; air-gap requirements.
- **Value proposition:** **On-Premise Air-Gapped AI Appliance** with a tuned LLM — data never leaves the network; backed by OSM AI Memory. Reflects OSM's military/defense heritage.
- **Hook:** "Elite autonomous security analysis — fully air-gapped, 100% on your infrastructure."
