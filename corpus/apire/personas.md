## Common objections (and APIRE's recommended response posture)

1. **"We can build this ourselves"** → Focus on time-to-market, opportunity cost, specialized expertise.
2. **"This will slow down our AI initiatives"** → Emphasize zero-touch deployment, performance benchmarks, policy flexibility.
3. **"We're not sure about our AI strategy yet"** → Security should be foundational, not retrofitted. Start small with pilot.
4. **"What about vendor lock-in?"** → APIRE enables multi-provider strategy, reduces LLM vendor lock-in.
5. **"How do we justify the cost?"** → ROI calculator, risk quantification, breach cost avoidance.
6. **"Our data is too sensitive to share"** → ZRA never trains on customer data; on-premise / private cloud options.
