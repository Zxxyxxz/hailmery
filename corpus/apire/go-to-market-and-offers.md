## GTM strategy — three pillars

1. **Dominate the Developer Experience** — Win technical implementers with frictionless 5-minute deployment and product-led growth.
2. **Champion the CISO's Compliance Mandate** — Become the go-to solution for CISOs in regulated EU industries with irrefutable NIS2 and EU AI Act compliance proof.
3. **Execute a Multi-Persona, Full-Funnel Campaign** — Awareness, qualified demand, and sales enablement tailored to Claudia, Ben, and Priya.

## Channel mix

- **Direct sales (primary)** — Enterprise and mid-market. AEs, Solutions Engineers, CSMs. POC-driven sales process.
- **Product-led growth (secondary)** — Self-serve trial for startups and scale-ups. Interactive demo, sandbox, upgrade path.
- **Channel partners (emerging)** — System Integrators (Big 4, Accenture), Cloud Marketplaces (AWS, Azure, Google), Technology Partners (SIEM, IAM, GRC), Resellers (international expansion).

## Funnel-stage offer strategy

### Awareness Stage (TOFU)
- **Goal:** Educate and build awareness
- **Gating:** Minimal (email only) or ungated
- **Format:** Checklists, guides, assessments
- **CTA:** Learn more, explore resources

### Consideration Stage (MOFU)
- **Goal:** Demonstrate expertise and solution fit
- **Gating:** Email + company + role
- **Format:** Whitepapers, webinars, case studies
- **CTA:** Request demo, start trial

### Decision Stage (BOFU)
- **Goal:** Enable purchase decision
- **Gating:** Full qualification form
- **Format:** ROI calculators, comparison guides, POC plans
- **CTA:** Talk to sales, start POC

## The 19 canonical lead magnets

### Awareness Stage (6 offers)

**1. AI Security Risk Assessment Tool** (interactive, ungated)
5-minute assessment across 6 dimensions: Threat Protection, Data Leakage, Policy Governance, Compliance, Multi-Provider, Cost Visibility. Outputs personalized risk score (0–100) + benchmarking + recommendations.

**2. The Complete Guide to NIS2 Compliance for AI Systems** (25–30 pages, email-gated)
NIS2 overview, affected organizations, technical/organizational measures, risk management, penalties, step-by-step compliance roadmap, checklist + templates.

**3. EU AI Act Readiness Checklist** (interactive + PDF, email-gated)
Risk classification (prohibited/high-risk/limited/minimal), transparency/documentation, human oversight, data governance, conformity assessment, post-market monitoring.

**4. OWASP Top 10 for LLMs Explained — Visual Guide** (infographic + 10-15p guide, ungated/minimal)
All 10 OWASP LLM risks: Prompt Injection, Insecure Output Handling, Training Data Poisoning, Model DoS, Supply Chain Vulnerabilities, Sensitive Information Disclosure, Insecure Plugin Design, Excessive Agency, Overreliance, Model Theft. Each with: plain explanation, real example, impact, how APIRE protects.

**5. AI Data Leakage Risk Calculator** (interactive, ungated)
Inputs: employees using AI, frequency, data types (PII/PHI/PCI/proprietary), current controls, industry. Outputs: % of prompts with sensitive data, breach risk, breach cost.

**6. "5-Minute AI Security" Video Series** (ungated, YouTube/LinkedIn)
Five 3–5min videos: What is Prompt Injection? · How AI Data Leakage Happens · NIS2 for AI Systems in 5 Min · Multi-Provider Governance Made Simple · Zero-Code AI Security: How It Works.

### Consideration Stage (6 offers)

**7. "The State of AI Security 2025" Research Report** (40–50p, email + company gated)
Survey-based original research: AI adoption trends, security incidents/breach costs, compliance challenges, budget allocation, technology stack, best practices, sector benchmarks.

**8. "Prompt Injection: The Complete Defense Guide" Whitepaper** (20–25p, email + company gated)
Direct vs. indirect prompt injection, technical explanation, real attack examples, why traditional tools fail, defense strategies, APIRE detection and blocking.

**9. "Multi-Provider AI Governance Playbook"** (30–35p, email + company gated)
Multi-provider governance challenges, framework/principles, policy design and enforcement, monitoring strategies, compliance mapping (NIS2/EU AI Act/GDPR), cost management, 30/60/90-day implementation roadmap, templates and checklists.

**10. Customer Case Studies Library** (4–6p each, ungated/minimal gate)
- Tier-1 European Bank (NIS2 compliance, 3-week deployment, 0 leakage, 40% cost reduction)
- Pan-European Healthcare Provider (HIPAA, real-time PHI masking, 5-min deployment)
- Series B SaaS Company (passed 10+ enterprise reviews, 0 PII incidents, 30% cost savings)
- *(All [UNVERIFIED] until confirmed — see `unverified-claims.md`.)*

**11. AI Security Maturity Model Assessment** (email + company gated)
5-level maturity model: Ad Hoc / Reactive / Defined / Managed / Optimized. Covers threat protection, data security, governance, compliance, multi-provider, cost optimization, team skills. Outputs maturity level + gap analysis + personalized roadmap + benchmarking.

**12. Monthly Webinar Series** (live, email-gated)
- NIS2 Compliance for AI Systems
- Defending Against Prompt Injection (live demos)
- Multi-Provider AI Governance
- AI Data Leakage: Hidden Risks

### Decision Stage (4 offers)

**13. ROI Calculator & Business Case Builder** (interactive + downloadable PPT, full qualification)
Cost savings (breach avoidance, compliance reduction, engineering time, security reviews, multi-provider optimization), revenue impact (TTM, customer trust, enterprise deals), risk reduction (quantified, insurance, fines). Outputs business case PPT, exec summary, 3-year financial projection, comparison vs. build/competitors.

**14. "APIRE vs. Alternatives" Comparison Guide** (15–20p, email + company gated)
APIRE vs. Building In-House (5 min vs. 6–12 months, TCO, maintenance), vs. Traditional DLP (AI-specific coverage), vs. Point Solutions (Lakera, Robust Intelligence), vs. Cloud Provider Native (multi-provider, lock-in).

**15. POC Success Kit** (sales-delivered)
POC objectives, timeline (typically 1–2 weeks), technical requirements, stakeholders, communication plan. Includes 5-min deployment guide, integration instructions (AI providers + SIEM + SSO), policy templates, test scenarios + attack simulations.

**16. Security & Compliance Documentation Package** (sales-delivered or gated)
SOC 2 Type II report, ISO 27001 cert, pen-test summary, security architecture whitepaper, DPA, subprocessor list, incident response plan, GDPR/NIS2/EU AI Act/HIPAA compliance docs, architecture diagrams, integration guides, API docs, security controls matrix, encryption & key management. *(Note: SOC 2 and ISO 27001 are flagged unverified.)*

### Product-Led Growth (3 offers)

**17. Free Trial — Self-Serve** (14-day, credit card required)
All core features, 1–2 AI providers, up to 100K API calls, email support, in-app guidance. Includes pre-configured demo environment, attack simulation, usage/cost analytics, upgrade prompts.

**18. Interactive Product Demo** (ungated)
Click-through demo with realistic data: Threat Detection / Data Masking / Policy Enforcement / Compliance Dashboard / Multi-Provider switching / Cost Analytics.

**19. Developer Sandbox** (GitHub public repo)
Docker Compose setup, sample apps (Python, Node.js, Go), pre-configured APIRE proxy, attack simulation scripts, policy examples, integration tests. Quick-start guide (5 minutes), API reference, integration patterns, best practices.

- "86% of companies had AI security incidents last year. Are you protected?"

**Pattern C — Contrast + Position:**
- "While legacy vendors stitch together acquired AI features, APIRE was built for the speed of AI development."

- "Stop AI Threats. Start Securing What Matters."
